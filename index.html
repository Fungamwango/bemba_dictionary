About us

English~Bemba dictionary is translated, designed and programmed by Chanda Fungamwango Mark a Software Engineer and teacher by profession. This web app is a learning tool targetted at anyone wishing to understand the meaning of english words in bemba and improve their vocabulary.

I invested my prominent time in the development of this amazing educational tool as i discovered that many learners especially those in rural based schools face a lot of challenges in the comprehesion of some english words and this hinders them from having a required interaction and participation in the learning process.

This dictionary consists of over 5,260 words with the transilation accuracy of approximately 95%. Each word is accompanied with an example both in english and bemba so as to cement the comprehesion. Am veraciously convinced that this tool will help you learn english in no time and begin speaking like a pro!.

Note: This dictionary is officially available on Bemba_dictionary where you get the interactive experience with additional features such as:

  • Personal quiz
  • Friend quiz challenge
  • Leaderboard
  • Word searching capability
  • Text to speech mechanism
  • And more!
